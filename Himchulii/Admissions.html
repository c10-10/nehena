Academic Programs at Himchuli Academy

We offer a wide range of academic programs designed to inspire learning and foster growth.

Primary School

The primary school program focuses on developing foundational skills in subjects such as Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies.

Secondary School

Our secondary school offers a broad curriculum, including advanced Mathematics, Science, Languages, and Arts, to prepare students for higher education.

High School

The high school program at Himchuli Academy provides students with in-depth knowledge and practical skills, especially in subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, and Computer Science.

Extracurricular Activities

In addition to academics, we offer a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, drama, and clubs, to enhance students' overall development.
